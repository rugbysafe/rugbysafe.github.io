Stage 1

Initial Rest (Body & Brain)

Objective

Recovery

Activity Allowed

No driving or exercise. Minimise screen time. Consider time off or adaption of study/work.

Duration

No training

% Max Heart Rate

No training

Stage 2a

Relative Rest (Symptom lmited activities)

Objective

Return to normal activities ( as symptoms permit).
No symptoms at the end of the 14 days

Activity Allowed

Initially daily activites that do not provoke symptoms. Consider time off or adaption of study/work

Duration

No training

% Max Heart Rate

No training

Stage 2b

Light aerobic exercise

Objective

Increase heart rate

Activity Allowed

Light jogging for 10-15 minutes, swimming or stationary cycling at low to moderate intensity. No resistence training

Duration

Less than 20 mins

% Max Heart Rate

Less than 70%

Stage 3

Sport-Specific Exercise

Objective

Add movement

Activity Allowed

For example:Running drills. No head impact activities

Duration

Less than 45 mins

% Max Heart Rate

Less than 80%

Stage 4

Non-contact Training

Objective

Exercise, coordination anf cognitive load. A return to learning/work must be achieved before returning to sport

Activity Allowed

For example:Passing drills. May start progressive resistance training

Duration

Less than 60 mins

% Max Heart Rate

Less than 90%
